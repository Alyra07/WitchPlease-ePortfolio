Witch, Please!

Witch, Please! is a shop simulation game set in a magical fantasy world. Players take on the role of a quirky young witch who recently opened her own enchantment workshop. Adventurers, townsfolk, and curious creatures visit the shop with unusual requests — from enchanted swords to cursed dolls.
The core gameplay revolves around interacting with customers, enchanting their items based on their personalities and stories, and navigating light puzzle challenges on the enchanting workbench. The game blends storytelling, and fantasy-themed enchanting mechanics in a cozy shop atmosphere.

Key Features

  • A main shop scene where customers arrive with their requests
  • Dialogue system: Four distinct customer requests and outcomes
  • Minigames for enchanting items on the Workbench: 3-4 unique tools with different functionalities
  • Cauldron
  • Magic Wand
  • Cleaning Cloth
  • Spellbook
  • UI navigation between dialogue (Customer Window), and enchanting Workbench interactions
  • Visual storytelling through character design and shop atmosphere (Game Audio)

Other Possible Features

  • Dialogue interaction system with branching dialogue options
  • Multiple endings based on player choices
  • Expanded lore and world-building elements (e.g. more customers, items, ...)
  • Simple progression system (e.g. day ends after all customers are served)
  • Reward system: Earning money from enchanting items (e.g. for unlocking new spells, tools)
  • Our own soundtrack

Story Outline

The game will have four different characters, each with their own unique storyline:

Wolf
A gruff, battle-worn warrior who barges into the shop with his dented sword. He demands the witch to reapply enchantments and clean the weapon. His tone is dismissive and arrogant, openly mocking the humble shop. He's a tough customer, expecting everything to be perfect without any patience or courtesy.

Raccoon
A curious and eccentric creature who stumbles upon the shop by accident. Enthusiastic about magic, he presents his personal pouch — claiming it holds his most valuable treasures. He insists the witch enchants it to make it forever secure but strictly warns her not to look inside. He speaks in fast, chaotic sentences, radiating chaotic energy.

Bird
A shy and gentle artist who heard about the shop from the talkative raccoon. The bird carries a single feather, which he hopes to have enchanted for his next art piece. Nervous and easily flustered, he struggles to articulate his request but is polite and kind-hearted.

Cat
A cunning and manipulative character. She arrives with a small voodoo doll, asking the witch to enchant it. Unknown to the witch at first, the cat plans to use the doll to control the witch herself. However, the wolf unexpectedly returns to the shop to intervene, creating a twist in the story.
